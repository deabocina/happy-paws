Payment Options

During the ordering process, you can choose one of the following payment methods:

CREDIT OR DEBIT CARD

Possibility to pay in installments with HPB and PBZ bank cards (1-6 installments):
  • ◦ American Express
  • ◦ Diners Club
  • ◦ MasterCard
  • ◦ Maestro
  • ◦ Visa
Payment is secure and encrypted.

HAPPY PAWS uses PayPal for online payments.

PayPal is a widely used and secure system for online payments, allowing real-time payments, as well as transactions through credit and debit cards, and other payment methods. PayPal ensures the secure entry and transfer of payment data for both buyers and merchants, supported by its compliance with industry security standards. PayPal uses advanced security measures, including SSL certificate encryption and the TLS 1.2 cryptographic protocol, to protect data during entry and transfer.

CASH ON DELIVERY

Payment in cash or by card to the courier upon package delivery.
  • ◦ Payment in cash to the courier. Cash on delivery is possible when ordering to all addresses on the continent and islands listed HERE. Cash on delivery is not possible for all other islands due to the nature of delivery.

  • ◦ One-time card payment to DPD or GLS courier. Upon package delivery, it is possible to make a one-time card payment to the courier.

  • ◦ DPD card payment - The package recipient will first receive a notice of the expected package delivery, followed by a notice containing a link to the WSPay system. The recipient has the option to make an online payment for the package using one of the following cards: Maestro, Mastercard, Visa, or Diners.

  • ◦ GLS card payment - Payment to the courier on a POS device upon package delivery.

BANK TRANSFER

When paying by bank transfer, it is necessary to provide personal and bank card information. Processing of this data is subject to the HAPPY PAWS privacy statement. The bank transfer for wire transfer orders must be made within 24 hours of placing the order. For transfers from foreign banks, it is necessary to check the fee charged by the bank for the transaction and pay the fee amount added to the order cost.
