Shipping

1. Orders over €35 are eligible for free shipping. For orders below €35, a delivery fee of €4 (VAT included) will be applied.

2. Delivery Time - Estimated delivery time is within 1-3 business days. Longer delivery times may occur during promotions and for remote locations. Bank transfer orders are submitted to the delivery service only after receiving confirmation of payment.

3. Longer delivery times may occur during web shop promotions.

4. Delivery is provided by the GLS courier service and is available throughout the territory of the Republic of Croatia, except for certain islands where HP delivers. Availability and delivery times to islands by GLS can be checked HERE. For the purpose of product delivery to the buyer, the company provides the buyer's personal data to the courier service in order to fulfill its obligations under this sales contract. The personal data provided include: name, surname, delivery address, and buyer's contact phone number.

5. The courier will notify the recipient via SMS about the delivery time, one day before delivery. If the courier cannot find the recipient at the specified delivery address, delivery will be attempted again on the 3rd and 5th day from the first delivery, with prior notice, after which the package will be returned if not collected. Delivery is made to the entrance of the residential building or to the nearest entrance accessible by the delivery vehicle. If it is a residential building, the courier is not obligated to carry the goods to the floor where the buyer is located but only to the entrance of the building. Check payment methods HERE.

6. The delivery cost is stated in the basket overview before payment. Orders over €35 are eligible for free delivery, applicable to delivery to one address. Products with a specified additional surcharge in the catalog are exempt from this, regardless of the total order amount. Any special surcharge (if applicable) is indicated for each individual item.
